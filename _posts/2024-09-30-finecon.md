### Regression Analysis
-------------
Regression is touted as the most reliable tool at econometricians' disposal. > Regression analysis is concerned with the study of the dependence of one variable, the dependent variable, on one or more other variables, the explanatory variables, to estimate and/or predict the (population) mean or average value of the former in terms of the known or fixed (in repeated sampling) values of the latter (_Gujarati, 2003_)
